TwitterFeed

Welcome to TwitterFeed, a site designed to keep you updated on world events through the contributions of you - the people of the world.

TwitterFeed takes the top 10 trends from regions around the world, and stores 50 of their latest tweets here. Be the first to know as Twitter users around the world voice their thoughts.

Worldwide

TwitterFeed covers 20 regions around the world to keep trends and information as broad as possible.

Fast

TwitterFeed updates every 30 minutes, 24/7, meaning the info here is as current as it gets.

Accessible

TwitterFeed is free and open to everyone - meaning this stream can be used by anyone at anytime.

THE WAY IT WORKS

Thanks to Twitter's public API, we can access tweet and trend information at any given time. By running a Python script 24/7, this data can be accessed and posted onto TwitterFeed all day long. That means more current info for the world and more awesomeness for you!
